Mango coconut chia pudding

Description

These Mango Coconut Chia Pudding cups are a fast and easy make-ahead breakfast with tons of fiber and protein.

Ingredients

  • 200 grams frozen mango chunks*
  • 1 330 grams can lite coconut milk*
  • 1 tsp sugar
  • 6 Tbsp chia seeds

Notes:

*Fresh mango can be used in place of frozen. You'll need about 2 cups of mango chunks.

**Do not use full-fat canned coconut milk as it has a lower moisture content and may not fully hydrate the chia seeds.

Steps

  1. If the mango chunks are in large pieces, chop them into smaller, 0.5-1cm pieces. Place half of the mango chunks , the coconut milk, and sugar in a blender and process until smooth.

  2. Divide the remaining mango chunks between 4 small containers (about the size of a cup). Add 1.5 Tbsp of chia seeds to each container. Finally, pour the mango coconut milk mixture into the containers, dividing it evenly between all four.

  3. Stir the contents of each container to make sure all the chia seeds have adequate exposure to the liquid. Place lids on the containers and refrigerate over night, or up to 4-5 days.

  4. Optional: you can add some chocolate cream or peanut butter for additional taste or nutritional value, and do not hesitate to experiment as this recipe is very forgiving.
